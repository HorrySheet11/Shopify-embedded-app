# My App Decisions during this take-home assignment

## Store Concept
Upon thinking of a store concept, I thought of making a PC parts and peripheral store since I am into PC and tech stuff. There I came up with the name "PC EZ" to be somewhat catchy and easy to remember but also to indicate that browsing and getting your PC needs are easy. 

## App Idea

In the app, I came up with a few concepts for the main feature of the app that I would be making. Initially, the main feature of the app would be an *inventory restocker* but I scratched that and just sticked with a **product rater**. You would be able to see all of your products that you have in your store and rate the products that you have imported/sell on your own.

## Key Architecture/schema decisions
I kept the schema simple for the product rating system. There is a product ratings table which has id , productId, productName, and rating columns. There would have also been a logs table where logs would have been stored and maybe a produts table where all of the products in your store would have been stored.

## Tradeoffs
The app is quite niche as it only focuses on giving ratings for your app. It is also not tied to your own store specifically.

## What I'd improve with more time
There are many things that I would improve if I had more time. But I mainly focused on the main feature of the app which was the rating system, and a few things in the theme. Nevertheless, I had fun making a project using tools that I have not yet encountered.
